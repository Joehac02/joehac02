Purpose

  

The purpose of this document is to provide me with a go to area to go to when I need to clarify who I am. My ideals, my ideas, my dreams, what keeps me going. It will all be stored here. This is for me to find who I am and get working towards a better me

Values

  • Truth: Do not lie. There is one exception. To hide who you are to those who are not ready.
  • Order: Put yourself in order before you work on others
  • Minimization: Wherever possible, dispose. If it does not bring you joy, you do not need it.
  • Hard Work: Towards my future picture.
  • Independence: Do not willingly submit yourself to tyranny.
  • Tyranny is defined as: The unwilling usperation of personal freedom by a malevolent force.
  • Faith: In humanity to do the right thing.

    Future Picture

     

    My future picture entails most of all being independent. Independent from the needs and wants of the world. I want to have fine tuned my life to the degree that I do not need the newest technology. Everything works as I need it to. I have enough food to eat every day. I want to have a stable job that I enjoy.
